About

Project Smiles is a tool for users to magically tranform a piece of text into one with a whole new meaning base on their wishes. This project aims to let people quickly and efficiently paraphrase words automatically by applying a set of templates to the text.

How to Start

First, you have to log in with your google account.

Secondly, select the template of your choosing by clicking on the radio buttons.

Thirdly, type in the text box, or copy and paste in a piece of text and click "Change".

Lastly, watch the magic work!

Sample Texts:

For Antonym Template: A sad man can never be happy because he is stupid enough to realise that this text is a clever text. One which is too deep for understanding.

For Simple Template An enraged man is always irritated and annoyed by any small provocations. He may have been a cheerful and joyous man, but he is now a dejected and bitter man.
